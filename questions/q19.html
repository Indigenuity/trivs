A Glossy Finish

When finishing wood, if you want a glossy, mirror-like shine, you have to polish it using friction. In traditional woodworking, this process of aggressively polishing a finish to a high gloss is known by what highly suggestive phrase?

Answer: Rubbing it out (accept "rubbing out the finish")
